About Brim Brokers

Premium custom headwear for businesses, teams, events, and brands since 2018.

Our Story

Brim Brokers started in a garage in Austin, Texas. What began as a side project helping local businesses brand their team with quality embroidered hats has grown into a full-scale custom headwear operation serving thousands of customers across the country.

We saw a gap in the market: businesses wanted premium custom hats without the hassle of minimum order requirements, confusing pricing, and long turnaround times. So we built something better — a simple, transparent process with fair pricing and fast delivery.

Our Mission

To make premium custom headwear accessible to everyone. Whether you need 1 hat or 10,000, we deliver the same attention to detail, quality materials, and responsive customer service.

Our Values

🏆

Quality First

We use commercial-grade embroidery machines and source only from top brands. Every hat is inspected before shipping.

🤝

Honest Pricing

No hidden fees, no setup charges, no surprise costs. Our pricing is transparent and volume discounts are automatic.

🚀

Fast & Reliable

Most orders ship within 7-10 days. Rush options available. We communicate every step of the way.
